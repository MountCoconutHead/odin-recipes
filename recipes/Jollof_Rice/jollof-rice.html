Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish with many regional variations.

It's made with a unique blend of spices, long-grain rice, and a tomato and red pepper base.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • 2 Fresh Tomatoes
  • 2 Fresh Pepper
  • 1 Red Bell pepper
  • 3 Onions
  • 1 Stock Cube
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2tsp Salt
