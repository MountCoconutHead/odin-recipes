Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish with many regional variations.

It's made with a unique blend of spices, long-grain rice, and a tomato and red pepper base.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato Paste
  • 2 Fresh Tomatoes
  • 2 Fresh Pepper
  • 1 Red Bell pepper
  • 3 Onions
  • 1 Stock Cube
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 1/2tsp Salt

Instructions

  1. Pour warm or hot water over the rice and allow it soak for about 30minutes
  2. Wash and blend, tomatoes, bell pepper, 2 onions, pepper and garlic, then par boil till water dries out
  3. Dice the remaining onion and set aside
  4. Heat up pot and add veg oil and then onions, sauté for about a minute then add tomatoes paste
  5. add the parboiled tomatoes paste Cooke for about 7 to 8 minutes stir in your salt and stock cube once the paste is cooked through
  6. Finally add your rice and water; turn to mix everything together and then cover pot with foil paper before closing the lid; reduce the flame at this pot and allow the low heat and steam to cook your rice
  7. Check once in while to make sure you don’t need extra water and taste so you can adjust the ingredients
  8. Remove from heat and serve with Grilled Chicken or something else
